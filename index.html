Who we are

About Boltic

We're a student-led FTC team turning sketches into machines, and rookies into engineers.

Boltic is a FIRST Tech Challenge team that designs, builds, and programs a competition robot from scratch every season. We do our own CAD, machining, wiring, and code, and we document the whole process the way real engineers do.

Beyond the robot, we run on gracious professionalism: we help other teams, share what we learn, and bring more students into STEM through outreach. Add a sentence here about how your team started and what you stand for.

What is FTC?

FIRST Tech Challenge puts students in grades 7–12 on teams that design, build, and program a robot to compete in a new game each year, while learning engineering, teamwork, and gracious professionalism.

How we're built

Meet the Team

Every robot is the work of several subteams pulling in the same direction.

Build & Mechanical

Designs, machines, and assembles the robot's structure and mechanisms.

Programming

Writes the autonomous routines, driver controls, and vision code.

CAD & Design

Models every part in CAD so the build team can fabricate with precision.

Outreach

Runs demos, mentors younger teams, and grows STEM in the community.

Business & Media

Handles sponsorship, the engineering notebook, and our online presence.

Drive Team

Operates the robot in matches and calls strategy on the field.
